Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Do I need to create an account to play?

No! You can play as a guest against AI opponents without signing up. However, creating an account (or signing in with Google) lets you save your progress, earn rankings, and access multiplayer features.

Is Ancient Board Games free?

Yes! All five games are completely free to play. We offer optional premium memberships and cosmetic items for players who want to support us and customize their experience.

What devices can I play on?

Ancient Board Games works in any modern web browser on desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. No downloads or installations required.

Gameplay

How do I learn to play these games?

Each game has a "Rules" button you can click before or during gameplay. We also have a comprehensive Rules page with detailed instructions for all five games.

What difficulty levels are available?

We offer three AI difficulty levels: Easy (great for learning), Medium (a fair challenge), and Hard (for experienced players). You can select your difficulty when starting a new game.

Can I play against friends?

Yes! You can create a private room and share the link with a friend, or use our matchmaking to find opponents. Multiplayer requires a free account.

Are these the real historical rules?

We've based our rules on the best available archaeological and historical evidence. For games like the Royal Game of Ur, we use rules decoded from ancient tablets. Where historical rules are uncertain, we've made educated choices that prioritize fun gameplay.

Accounts & Progress

How does the ranking system work?

We use an ELO-based rating system. You start at 1200 points. Winning against higher-rated opponents earns more points, while losing to lower-rated opponents costs more. Each game has its own separate rating.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact us at support@ancientboardgames.io and we'll delete all your data within 30 days.

Premium & Shop

What does Premium membership include?

Premium members enjoy an ad-free experience, exclusive board skins, priority matchmaking, and a special profile badge. Premium is $4.99/month or $39.99/year (save 33%).

What are coins used for?

Coins are our in-game currency used to purchase cosmetic items like board skins, piece designs, and avatars. Coins don't affect gameplay — they're purely for customization.

Can I get a refund?

Premium subscription refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if you haven't used premium features. Coin purchases are non-refundable once coins are spent. Contact support@ancientboardgames.io for refund requests.

Technical

The game isn't loading. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R for hard refresh), 2) Clear your browser cache, 3) Try a different browser, 4) Check if JavaScript is enabled. If problems persist, contact us.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate bug reports! Email us at support@ancientboardgames.io with a description of what happened, what browser you're using, and any error messages you saw.

Contact

How can I contact you?

Email us at support@ancientboardgames.io for technical issues, or contact@ancientboardgames.io for general inquiries. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.

Can I suggest a new game to add?

Absolutely! We're always researching ancient games to potentially add. Send your suggestions to contact@ancientboardgames.io. We especially love hearing about games with good historical documentation.
